that will help fight regular stress and pressure as what we now-a-days understand as "Music/ Sound Therapy". The joyfull UI and pressence of the app, makes you feel that you are 
always accompanied by a true spiritual, joyful and anxiety reliever. The app contains soulful music, sound vibration effects, bedtime stories and routines for regular meditation,
all of which are able to combat your daily life byproducts. And the best part! all of this is free and will be served according to your mood. Medihealer will be along side you
